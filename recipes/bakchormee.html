Bak Chor Mee (Minced Meat Noodles)

Description

Bak chor mee is a savoury noodle dish found in Singapore and Malaysia. The fragrance of the mushrooms and various meat are complemented by the spice from the sambal chili, making for a very flavourful meal. This recipe will help you to make this delicacy from scratch at home!

Ingredients

  • Mee pok (flat egg noodles) or mee kia (thin egg noodles)
  • Sambal chili
  • Chinese meatballs and fishballs
  • Minced pork, pork slices and pork liver
  • Mushrooms
  • Crispy fried lard
  • Crispy fried shallots
  • Chinese black vinegar

Steps

  1. Marinate the minced meat, meat slices and liver overnight.
  2. Soak the mushrooms overnight, then cook them for an hour the next day.
  3. On the next day, to make the soup, boil the meat bones for a little over an hour with some aromatics.
  4. To make the sauce for the noodles, mix the sambal paste, liquid from the mushrooms, some soup and black vinegar for 2 minutes.
  5. Blanch the noodles and beansprouts.
  6. At the same time, cook the meatballs and fishballs in the soup.
  7. While the balls are cooking, cook the minced meat, meat slices and liver in the same soup.
  8. Mix the sauce, noodles and beansprouts in a bowl.
  9. Top with all of the meat and the balls.
  10. Optional: Ladle some soup over.
  11. Top with stewed mushrooms and liquid.
  12. Final garnish with lard and shallots.
