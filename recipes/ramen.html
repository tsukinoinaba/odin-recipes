Miso Ramen

Description

Spaghetti aglio e olio is a classic Italian dish, notable for its fragrance due to the use of garlic and olive oil. This recipe is a beginner-friendly way to recreate its authentic flavour.

Ingredients

  • Miso
  • Bean paste
  • Sesame seeds and sesame oil
  • Chicken broth
  • White pepper powder
  • Ramen noodles
  • Chashu
  • Ajitsuke tamago (eggs marinated in soy sauce)
  • Menma (seasoned bamboo shoots)
  • Shiraga Negi (julienned white negi/leeks)
  • Narutomaki (Japanese fish cakes)
  • Sweet corn
  • Chopped green onion
  • Nori seaweed

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti.
  2. Toast the garlic in olive oil.
  3. Stir the seasonings into the pasta, then stir in the garlic and oil.
  4. Toss with cheese.
